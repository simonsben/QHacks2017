What is Flag?

Flag aims to change the way we interact with and receive service in our daily lives. Whether you’re asking a question or asking for assistance, our easy-to-use interface ensures you’ll be well taken care of as soon as possible.



    


How can you use it?

From restaurants to schools to hospitals, the Flag system seamlessly integrates into any workflow. Businesses can use Flag to eliminate the headache of managing and organizing simple tasks.



Why Flag?

Flag was created out of personal need by four engineering and computing students. They felt that the whiteboard-based queuing system used in their labs to beckon TAs was in dire need of a revamp. An online version of this outdated system provides convenience and scalability for students and educators alike.
